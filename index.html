Yoga · Stretch · Movement

Move with intention,

I'm Emily Barrera — a movement teacher and artist. I guide yoga and stretch classes, and create through choreography and dramaturgy. Whether you're finding length in your body or shaping a piece for the stage, it all begins with breath.

What I offer

Yoga

Grounded, breath-led classes for strength, balance, and calm — welcoming to every level.

Stretch

Deep, restorative mobility work to open the body, release tension, and move freely.

Choreography

Movement crafted for the stage — collaborating with dancers and performers to bring ideas to life.

Dramaturgy

Shaping the story and structure of a work — the thinking behind the movement.

A practice, a performance, a play

My work lives where the studio meets the stage. In class, I help people reconnect with their bodies — softening, strengthening, and breathing. In the theatre, that same attention becomes choreography and dramaturgy.
