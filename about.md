I'm Emily Barrera, a movement teacher and artist. My days move between the studio and
the stage: teaching yoga and stretch classes, and creating work as a choreographer and
dramaturg.

I believe the body holds our stories. In class, I help people slow down, breathe, and
rediscover ease and strength in how they move. In my creative work, that same curiosity
becomes choreography — shaping how bodies speak in space — and dramaturgy, the thinking
that gives a piece its shape and meaning.

### A little more

- **Teaching** — yoga and stretch classes for all levels, in-person and by arrangement.
- **Choreography** — creating and collaborating on movement for performance.
- **Dramaturgy** — supporting the development and structure of new work.
